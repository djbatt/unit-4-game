Game Rules

At the beginning of game the computer generates you a random number!
The crystals below all have a random value, and clicking one will add it's value to your score!
Your goal is to get your score to match the random number, only using the four gems below!
At the start of the game each crystal's value is randomized, and its value is hidden until you click on it!
